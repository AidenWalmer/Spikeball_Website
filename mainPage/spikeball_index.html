General Club Information:

Welcome to the Spikeball Club! The goal of Spikeball Club is to provide a stress-free environment for college students to engage in casual and competitive matches of Spikeball. The club strives to promote a healthy atmosphere for physical exercise on campus while facilitating a community to build interpersonal relationships. We welcome any currently enrolled students of Elizabethtown College to come out and play, no matter your skill level.

We meet every Friday from 4:00pm-6:00pm on Brinser Field, as long as weather permits. We will send out an email to all current members notifying you if we will not be meeting.
